CryptKeep Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

CryptKeep ("the app") is a zero-knowledge password manager. It lets you store and manage vault entries (titles, usernames, passwords, URLs, notes, and categories). All sensitive vault data is encrypted on your device using AES-256-CBC with a key derived from your master password via PBKDF2-SHA256 (100,000 iterations). Your master password never leaves your device.

The app requires an account (email and master password) to function. Your email is used for authentication only. Encrypted vault data is synced to Supabase, a third-party backend service, so you can access your vault across devices. Because encryption and decryption happen entirely on your device, the server only ever receives and stores encrypted data — it cannot read your passwords or vault contents.

The app does not collect analytics, telemetry, or usage data. It does not use advertising SDKs or share data with third parties beyond what is described above. The only network communication is between the app and Supabase for authentication and encrypted vault sync.

If you lose your master password, your vault cannot be recovered — by design, no one (including the developer) can decrypt your data. You can delete your account and all associated encrypted data by contacting the developer.

If the app is obtained through the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may process data related to download, purchase, and licensing under Microsoft's own policies; this policy describes only processing performed by the CryptKeep app.
